Monash University

About

Monash is the largest university in Melbourne with an enviable reputation in world class education. Discover more about who we are & what we stand for.

Monash University is a public research university based in Melbourne, Australia. Named for prominent World War I general, Sir John Monash, it was founded in 1958 and is the second oldest university in the State of Victoria. The university has a number of campuses, four of which are in Victoria, and one in Malaysia. More details.
