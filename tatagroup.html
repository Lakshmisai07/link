Tata Group

About company

Tata Group is an Indian multinational conglomerate holding company headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. Founded in 1868 by Jamsetji Tata, the company gained international recognition after purchasing several global companies. One of India's largest conglomerates, Tata Group is owned by Tata Sons.

Address:

Tata Group India offices are located in Mumbai , Maharastra and Pun.e

Recruitment Process

The Tata Communications recruitment process mainly consists of 4 rounds. They are:

Written Test

Tata communications test pattern consists of two sections - Aptitude and Technical. The number of questions and time duration of the test are given below.

Group Discussion

The mass eliminating round of Tata Communications Recruitment Process is Group Discussion and the purpose is to assess the managerial ability of the candidate. This round is the gateway to the final interview round and thus has high attrition rates. There are a certain set of skills that are evaluated in a group discussion and they are Communication Skills, Leadership SKills,Rational Thought Process, General AWarenessn and Group Behavior

Technical Interview

Tata Communications career for fresher in engineering department requires one to be sound with the technical concepts. To assess this Tata Communications Recruitment process consists of 1- 2 rounds of interview with basic questions like your project works to complicated programs. Hence the candidates should have a fair knowledge about their core subject and programming languages (at least C programming).

HR interview

This is the final round of Tata Communications Recruitment process and the easiest one. It consists of basic questions like introduce yourself which is conducted to understand the candidate personally.
